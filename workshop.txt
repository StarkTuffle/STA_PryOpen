description=[list][*]Pry open locked doors, windows, and vehicles using a crowbar. (Compatibile with modded crowbars)

description=[*]Dynamic consequences like hand injuries, alarm triggers, and tool wear.
description=[*]Supports both context menu and radial interactions for smooth gameplay.
description=[*]Fully customizable through sandbox settings and mod options.[/list]
